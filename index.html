About Me

I was born and grew up in Milan, Italy. In 2020 I have moved to Massachusetts, where I am studying Engineering with a major in Robotics at Olin College of Engineering: a top-tier engineering college specialising in project-based learning, ranked 2nd in the 2023 U.S. News & World Report for Best Undergraduate Engineering Schools. Here, I am participating every day in team-based, hands-on learning through a variety of projects, ranging from computational robotics to entrepreneurial venture capital campaigns. I consider myself to be a highly goal-oriented and entrepreneurial individual with the ambition to take up an international career. I am striving towards leaving a lasting impact in the robotics industry. I've led several youth groups, academic teams, project teams. I consider myself to be a quick learner and very adaptable to changes around me.
